Program View C

CSULB Electrical Engineering transfer breakdown

Interpreted as CSULB Electrical Engineering (BSEE). CSULB also offers a separate Electronics Engineering Technology program, but this section is built around the BSEE path.

Admission minimum

CSULB lists a minimum 2.5 GPA for transfer consideration in Electrical Engineering.

Major-specific admission prep

MATH 122, MATH 123, and PHYS 151, which map most closely to MATH 170, MATH 190, and PHYS 201.

Recommended extra prep

PHYS 152 is recommended for transfer applicants, and the department also lists EE 186 as additional lower-division preparation for CSULB students.
